MIDI sketching for Digitone, Digitakt, and TD-3

Start with a musical direction, not an empty grid.

Signal Rack generates a related four-bar idea across the rack, then gives you a focused set of controls for changing the rhythm, harmony, density, and movement.

Inside the rack

A small collection of tools that work on the same phrase.

01

Phrase Generator

Choose a root, harmonic color, style, energy, phrase shape, and leader. Signal Rack writes related material into all eleven lanes.

02

Lane generators

Apply Euclidean rhythms or phrase-aware arpeggios to a selected lane without regenerating everything else.

03

Modulation

Route eight clocked LFOs—including drawn curves—to cutoff, delay, and Digitone octave over periods from a quarter note to 128 bars.

04

Scenes

Move the rack together through eight useful density states, from the full arrangement to isolated drums, melody, tops, or a drop.

05

Direct editing

Open any note or trig when the generated result needs a specific correction. Each lane can use its own length, groove, chance, and timing.

06

Generator Lab

Run blind listening batches on the hardware, record structured feedback, and export complete sessions for focused generator improvement.

Working method

Get to a playable proposition quickly.

  1. Choose

    Set the tonal center, vocabulary, rhythmic identity, energy, and four-bar behavior.

  2. Generate

    Create one connected idea for bass, harmony, puncture, acid line, and seven drum roles.

  3. Shape

    Use lane generators, scenes, modulation, and direct edits where the phrase needs them.

  4. Perform

    Move density, mutes, and parameters while the native MIDI engine keeps the rack in sync.

Quick setup

Connect the instruments you want to use.

Digitone

Select its MIDI output, match tracks 1–3 to their channels, and enable note plus CC/NRPN reception for cutoff and delay.

Read the Digitone setup

Digitakt

Load kick, snare, closed hat, open hat, rimshot, clap, and texture Sounds on tracks 1–7, then enable note reception.

Read the Digitakt setup

TD-3

Select its MIDI output and receive channel. Signal Rack generates one monophonic acid lane with velocity accents and legato note-overlap slides.

Read the TD-3 setup
